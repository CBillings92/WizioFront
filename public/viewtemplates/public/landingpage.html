What is Wizio?

Founded in January of 2015, Wizio utilizes cutting-edge technology to offer an automated realtor service that connects quality tenants to landlords and property managers faster and cheaper than ever before. The software as a service provides virtual reality apartment showings, automated lease generation and submission, and tenant screening. Wizio aims to enhance the process for everyone by bringing affordable automation to the apartment rental market.
