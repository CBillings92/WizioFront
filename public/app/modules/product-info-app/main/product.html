Frequently Asked Questions

Can virtual tours be watermarked?

Yes! We have a watermarking feature available for putting your brokerage name or other text as a watermark on the tour.


Can you “walk around” in tours?

Yes! We are adding a navigation feature as one of several new tools for our product launch! Expect it soon.

Can you syndicate virtual tours to MLS?

Yes! Agents and brokerages relying on MLS Pinnergy will be able to cross-syndicate virtual tours with their listings. Interested in syndicating to another platform? Let us know at support@wizio.co.


Does each virtual tour receive a unique URL?

Each virtual tour you create has its own unique URL tied to that specific tour.


I’m worried about other agents or individuals stealing my links or the content I’ve created, what steps are you taking to prevent this?

At Wizio, we work hard to protect the content and tours you spend your time creating. You’ll notice, if you try to save a photo directly from a tour it simply won’t work. In addition, you can password protect tours to prevent unauthorized access, add your contact information via the agent profile feature, and watermark your tour.


Is it difficult to make a tour?

Our goal at Wizio is to make sure that making a virtual tour takes no more than 10 minutes (in most cases, our clients spend about 5 minutes per property). This means we need to keep our process as simple as possible. We’re happy to provide a demo to prove that point. Contact us at info@wizio.co to learn more.


Are there limits on how many tours I can create?

As of right now, no. In the future we may add a premium subscription for high-powered users (think 100+ tours). We’re not interested in nickeling and diming you for every tour you create.


Can I add additional users to my account?

Under the office subscription, you can have a multi-user account.


If I’m having an issue with my account, who can I contact?

Please email support@wizio.co or call 617-297-7472. We’re here to help you succeed.


I have a question that’s not answered here, who should I send it to?

We’re always looking for new questions to add to the FAQ. Email us at support@wizio.co.
