Frequently Asked Questions
What does a finished tour look like?

Check out our demo.

What cities can I request tours in?

We can now serve the Greater D.C. and Boston markets, and are in the process of expanding

Booking
How can I book a virtual tour?

We require users to create an account before booking tours. You can do so here, by calling 617-297-7472, or emailing support@wizio.co .

How far in advance do I have to book a virtual tour?

Book a virtual tour for tomorrow or 6 months from now. We work with your schedule to meet your needs.

Are there certain hours you are available to film properties?

Our photographers are available 7 days a week from 7am-8pm!

Can I reschedule a tour?

Yes! Simply choose a new time by modifying your existing booking through the email confirmation.

Process
How long does it take to create a virtual tour?

Our photographers typically spend between 30 and 45 minutes per listing. This can vary slightly by the size of the property.

Do I need to be present while the photographer is filming?

No. As long as our photographers are given access to the space (given a key or otherwise), you do not need to be present.

When will I receive access to my virtual tour?

The virtual tour will be delivered to your Wizio account within 24 hours after the photographer has visited the property.


Who are your photographers?

We employ contracted photographers that are trained on using 360-degree equipment and the Wizio platform.

Payment and Pricing
When will my credit card be charged?

A hold is put on your credit card at the time of booking. You will be charged after the virtual tour is delivered to you.


What is your cancellation policy?

If you cancel your virtual tour film session, you must do so 24 hours in advance. Inside of 24 hours you will be charged a $50 cancellation fee. You can reschedule by changing the booking in your confirmation email.


Who are your photographers?

We employ contract photographers that are trained on using 360-degree equipment and the Wizio platform. Our contractors are fully covered by insurance.

Product
Can you syndicate virtual tours to MLS?

Yes! Agents and brokerages relying on MLS Pinnergy will be able to cross-syndicate virtual tours with their listings. Interested in syndicating to another platform? Simply add your tour link in the “Virtual Tour Link” field when updating your MLS listing. Please note: if you have added contact information to the tour you may need to contact us (support@wizio.co) to remove the email and additional information in order to make it MLS-compliant.


Do you offer any additional features?

We are developing a suite of premium add-ons that customers can add to their virtual tour experience. Interested in learning more? Contact us at support@wizio.co


Can you “walk around” in tours?

Yes! We have added a walk-through experience similar to Google Street View.


Does each virtual tour receive a unique URL?

Each virtual tour you create has its own unique URL tied to that specific tour.
