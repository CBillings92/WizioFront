A Tenant movement
for change.

Wizio’s mission is to bring honest, transparent information to the apartment market in Boston
by utilizing crowd sourced videos and lease automation.

The Four Principles
of Wizio

  1. Our homes should be our homes.

    The Wizio platform seeks to bring more accountability by working with landlords & city officials to develop a system that helps to maintain homes at standards dictated by law.

  2. Freedom of information.

    Information should be upfront and free to access.
    Our team believes that complete transparency is a right
    & we are working hard to make sure that happens.

    3.  

  3. People come first. No Doubt.

    Whether it is a prospective tenant, landlord or employee, Wizio exists to serve. Our personal experiences with Boston’s flawed apartment process drive us to create positive change.

  4. We can always be better.

    We are motivated by the terrible state of the home rental process and an intrinsic desire to provide a better service. Wizio can always be be better for our communities.
